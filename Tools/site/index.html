Why napkin

Architecture the compiler can prove.

01

Clean isolation, by the compiler

Business logic lives in final actor interactors. Routing and presentation are @MainActor. Swift enforces the dependency rule — not convention, not lint rules.

02

No Combine. No RxSwift.

@Observable, AsyncStream, and structured concurrency replace the reactive stack. Combine is removed entirely; cancellation comes from Task.

03

Built for iOS 26 & macOS 26

Uses isolated deinit, Mutex from Synchronization, @Observable, and the new Observations { } block. Swift 6.2 throughout.

04

Modeled on Uber's RIBs

Builder, Component, Interactor, Router, Presenter. The familiar hierarchical architecture — rebuilt from the ground up around Swift Concurrency.
